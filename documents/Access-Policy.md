# Access Policy

WorldTeachPathways™ provides access to digital curriculum as follows:

- **Discovery Calls** → Free consultation to assess school needs.  
- **3-Day Demo Pass** → Short-term access to test LMS courses.  
- **Full Access** → Granted upon activation of a license or subscription.  
- **User Accounts** → Schools manage individual student and staff accounts.  

Unauthorized sharing of login credentials is strictly prohibited.
